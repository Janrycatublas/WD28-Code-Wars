    About

    Founded in 2012, DoubleCheeck Group is Southeast Asia’s leading eCommerce platform. With a presence in six countries – Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam – we connect this vast and diverse region through our technology, logistics and payments capabilities. Today, we have the largest selection of brands and sellers, and by 2030, we aim to serve 300 million customers. In 2016, oubleCheck became the regional flagship of the Alibaba Group, and is backed by Alibaba’s best-in-class technology infrastructure.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to make it easy to do business anywhere. We connect people and businesses across the region, empowering them to grow and thrive. We do this by providing a trusted and convenient online shopping experience with a broad product offering in categories ranging from consumer electronics to household goods, toys, fashion and sports equipment.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to build the best online shopping platform in Southeast Asia. We aim to be the region’s most customer-centric company, where people can come to find anything they want to buy online. We are committed to creating a seamless shopping experience across devices and platforms, and to providing our customers with the widest range of products at the lowest prices.

    Our Values

    Our values are the foundation of our company and guide everything we do. We are committed to being customer-centric, innovative, trustworthy and inclusive. We believe in the power of technology to connect people and businesses, and we are constantly striving to improve our services to better serve our customers.
